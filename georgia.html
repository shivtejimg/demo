Duration of MBBS in GEORGIA
  • Duration of course for MBBS in GEORGIA is mainly 6 years, however, some universities are providing courses of even 6 years .
  • For the ﬁrst few years of the course theoretical classes are focused and generally reserved for the academic program and complete theory is taught within 5 years .
The next year / ﬁnal year is reserved for the internship period in which the main focus is on practical knowledge which in turn enables the students to understand the subject in a better way and let them become more conﬁdent in the end of completing their course.
  • Indian students are having options to do internship in India as well as in GEORGIA whichever they want , the only condition for internship is that Indian students who want to practice in India after completing MBBS from GEORGIA have to clear the NExT examination with minimum passing marks conducted by National Medical Commission i.e NMC.

Students should be at least 17 years old at the time of application and must have a minimum of 55% in higher education in the general category with physics , chemistry and biology as main subjects.
  • Financial background of guardians must be solid enough to cover the entire course fees of MBBS in GEORGIA .

Admission Process for MBBS in GEORGIA
Admissions into GEORGIA university is easy and feasible for every student . Remarkably , the admission process is less-time consuming with a high standard level of education. Here are some important points about the GEORGIA admission process.
  1. Online application form must be ﬁlled by the students itself along with complete scanned documents .
  2. Once the scanned documents are veriﬁed, admission letters will be sent to the shortlisted students by the university.
  3. The admission letter should be registered and must be approved by NMC.
  4. Once the process is completed by the student end, then we at ENSURE EDUCATION apply for a visa and it will take around 30-40 days for the complete process.
  5. Tuition- Fees must be paid by the student themselves directly to the bank account of University.

Required Documents for MBBS in GEORGIA :
Following are the documents which are mandatory during the admission process in any university of GEORGIA.
  1. Scorecard of NEET ( Candidate must have qualiﬁed NEET exam in the required category ).
  2. Marksheet of secondary (10th) and higher secondary education (12th) and must have scored a minimum of 50% in PCM.
  3. Transfer Certificate ( TC ) from the higher school.
  4. Valid INDIAN PASSPORT with ﬁve passport size photos.
  5. Character certificate from the school.
  6. Bank account statements must be submitted to ensure the capacity of students towards the repayment of tuition fees.
  7. PCC i.e Police Clearance Certificate is required for the submission.
  8. NEGATIVE HIV - AIDS report is Mandatory.
  9. Medical Insurance with full medical report.
